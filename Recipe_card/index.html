Spaghetti Bolognese

An easy classic Spaghetti Bolognese recipe, made in 30mins!

Ingredients

  • Mince meat (beef or lamb), 400g
  • Spaghetti pasta, 400g
  • 1 onion
  • Chopped tomatoes, 1 tin
  • Garlic, 1 clove
  • Fresh parsley, handful
  • Italian mixed herbs
  • Olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Step 1 - Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to frying pan and put it on a low heat
  2. Step 2 - Chop 1 onion and add it to the frying pan, saute the onions for 1-2 mins
  3. Step 3 - In a separate pan, add water and put it on a high heat for it to boil
  4. Step 4 - Add the mince meat to the frying pan, and put the heat on medium
